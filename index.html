Frequently Asked Questions

How does solar energy work?
Solar panels capture sunlight with photovoltaic (PV) cells, converting it into electricity. An inverter then changes it into usable power for your home, business, or appliances.
Will solar panels really save me money?
Yes. By producing your own electricity, you can cut monthly bills significantly. With government subsidies and loans, your return on investment is even faster.
Is my home or business suitable for solar?
Most properties are suitable. You need enough roof space with good sun exposure and minimal shade. New Solar Energy offers a free site assessment to check feasibility.
How long do solar systems last?

Do solar panels work during cloudy or rainy days?
Yes. Panels still generate electricity in cloudy conditions, although output is reduced. Even in less sunny weather, youâ€™ll continue saving on energy costs.
